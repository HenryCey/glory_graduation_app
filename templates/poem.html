✍ A Poem for You

✨ Every word here was written with you in mind — click the poem below to read the whole piece ✨

“

Behind quiet eyes, a story hides,

Of battles fought and tears that dried.

A heart once broken, now made strong,

By grace that carried you all along...

You wear a smile, though the world may not see,

The depth of your kindness, your quiet beauty.

Gentle and funny, yet stronger than most,

Your light shines brightest when life tries to boast.

Though shy at first, your soul is wide,

A place where laughter and care reside.

Not all can know the treasure you are,

But God does — and He’s brought you far.

So here’s to the journey, the healing, the cheer,

To brighter tomorrows, to love without fear.

You’re more than the pain, more than the past,

You’re a friend worth keeping, a joy that will last.

Continue winning and shine on my dear friend {Glory Ejiogu}
